Tiramisu

Ingredients

  • 4 large egg
  • 100 grams granulated sugar
  • 1 cup Mascarpone
  • 3/4 cups espresso or coffee
  • 2 tbs rum
  • 2 tbs cocoa powder
  • 200 gram package ladyfingers

Steps

  1. Separate the egg whites from the egg yolks
  2. mix the egg whites and half of the sugar in bain marie
  3. whip the mix until steady peaks
  4. mix the other half of the sugar with the egg yolks
  5. add the mascarpone to the egg yolks sugar mix and mix it together
  6. add the egg whites sugar mix to the mascarpone mix in steps
  7. soak one side of the ladyfingers in the coffee and place one layer into a ceramic or glas cooking pan
  8. spread the mascarpone cream on top of the ladyfingers
  9. keep adding layers in this manner
  10. let the tiramisu rest in the fridge for at least 8 hours
  11. take it out and add a layer of cocoa powder before serving
  12. enjoy!
