Coxinha

Ingredients

  • 500 g chicken breast
  • 2 cup chicken broth
  • 1 tbs butter
  • 2 cups flour

Steps

  1. Cook the chicken breast with the chicken broth in a large pot
  2. saute the chicken with species
  3. Add to another pan the butter and two cups of the water where you cooked the chicken
  4. Add to the mix butter/water the flour in steps
  5. let everything cool down and start modelling and filling the coxinhas
  6. enjoy!
